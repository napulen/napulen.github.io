PhD Candidate in Music Technology

Biography

I am interested in all of the ways in which modern technologies can help us to learn, understand, create, and enjoy music.

Interests

  • Computational Music Theory
  • Music Information Retrieval
  • Deep Learning
  • Symbolic and Audio Music Representations
  • Game Development

Education

  • PhD in Music Technology, 2022

    McGill University, Montréal

  • MSc in Sound and Music Computing, 2017

    Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Barcelona

  • Licenciatura en Informática, 2013

    Universidad de Guadalajara, México
